Our Story

A Love Letter
to Coffee

Born in 2015 from a shared passion for the perfect cup, Brew & Bloom started as a small corner shop with a big dream. Today, we source beans directly from family farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — roasting them in-house to bring out flavors you won't find anywhere else.

Every morning, our baristas arrive before sunrise to bake pastries from scratch and dial in the perfect espresso. It's not just coffee — it's a ritual, a community, and a moment of calm in a busy world.
